Legacy

Why Shooting Ball?

Unlike traditional volleyball, Shooting Ball is a test of raw power and lightning-fast reflexes. It is one of India's most beloved rural sports that has now transitioned into a professional national-level discipline. Nandakhal Youth Foundation is proud to bring this heritage to the center stage in 2026.
